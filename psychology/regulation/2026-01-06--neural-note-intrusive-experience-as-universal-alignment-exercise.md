## Phase A — Event Embedding (Immediate, Lossless Capture)

### 1. Observable Facts

- A realization occurred that any intrusive experience (thoughts, sensations, urges, dissociation, shame, doubt) can be reframed at the moment it appears as an exercise in alignment with the chosen self model.
- No attempt is made to suppress, resolve, analyze, or eliminate the intrusive material.
- The sole action taken is choosing a response consistent with the healthy / chosen self model.
- This choice is made independent of affect, certainty, or symptom intensity.

### 2. Internal State Markers

- A marked sense of clarity and “click” when recognizing that this applies universally, without exceptions.
- Relief arising not from symptom reduction, but from regaining authorship over meaning.
- Felt shift from reacting to internal content → acting from chosen identity.
- Recognition that even severe or persistent symptoms no longer imply failure or regression.

### 3. Pattern Signals Detected

- Authorship Reassignment: Meaning is assigned by response, not by content.
- Feedback Loop Severance: Intrusions no longer reinforce the negative self model.
- Identity-First Processing: Self model precedes affect, rather than being determined by it.
- Universal Generalization: No need for symptom-specific strategies.
- Fuel Inversion: Outputs of the negative self model are repurposed as training material.

### 4. Interpretive Hypotheses (Explicitly Marked)

- Intrusive phenomena are DMN-generated outputs, not directives.
- Reinforcement occurs only when meaning is granted at the identity level.
- Treating intrusions as alignment exercises prevents consolidation of the negative self model.
- This mechanism may represent a general solution across OCD, CPTSD, DP/DR, motivation collapse, and identity instability.
- Confidence level: High, based on internal coherence and immediate functional impact.

### 5. Outcome / State Transition

- Shift from symptom-focused coping to identity-anchored response selection.
- Decreased urgency to “fix” internal experience.
- Increased stability independent of momentary state.
- Intrusions lose their ability to dictate trajectory.
