---

Note: Confidence as Behavioral Allegiance (DP/DR Consolidation Phase)

Core Insight

At this stage of recovery, confidence is not a felt state and should not be targeted as one. Attempting to feel confident reinforces DP/DR, because DP/DR is defined by mistrust of internal experience.

Confidence is instead a behavioral stance:

- A choice to stop negotiating with doubt
- A refusal to re-evaluate ownership based on transient feelings
- An allegiance to a self-model already selected

Confidence = non-participation in doubt arbitration, not emotional certainty.


---

Model Divergence & Why DP/DR Temporarily Increases

What is occurring is not regression, but self-model divergence:

- Old self-model: still active, contains beliefs like “this isn’t real,” “don’t trust ownership,” “something is wrong.”
- New self-model: increasingly coherent, grounded in accumulated evidence, insight, and continuity.

As the new model strengthens, the contrast between the two models becomes more salient.
This contrast manifests as:

- Increased DP/DR
- Unreality
- Tension
- A sense that something is “off”

This does not mean the new model is wrong.
It means both models cannot coexist, and pruning has not finished.

Pruning occurs through non-engagement, not debate.


---

Why “Checking” Is the Final Trap

Rechecking, re-analyzing, or weighing whether the model is real:

- Hands authority back to the old self-model
- Assumes doubt deserves arbitration
- Keeps both models co-activated
- Prevents consolidation

The issue is not the content of doubt, but the act of engaging with it.

At this stage, checking = reinforcement of the old model.


---

Nature of “Moments of Truth”

Moments where ownership feels real, grounded, and obvious are sampling events, not fragile states to preserve.

They demonstrate:

- The new model is accessible
- Alignment between belief, context, and self
- DP/DR can drop without effort

Trying to hold onto these moments increases interference.
Their function is proof of existence, not permanence.

Correct interpretation:

> “This state exists. My system knows how to access it. Time and non-interference will do the rest.”


---

Why Acceptance Is the Only Forward Lever

At this phase:

- Effort increases noise
- Reassurance feeds doubt
- Analysis sustains dual activation

Acceptance here means:

- Allowing sensations, doubt, and disparity to exist
- Refusing to engage in resolution-seeking
- Withholding participation in the old model’s protest behavior

This is not passivity.
It is strategic non-engagement.


---

Practical Orientation

The task is not to win against doubt.
The task is to outlast it.

A minimal anchor (not a mantra, not a ritual):

> “I’ve already chosen. The rest is settling.”

No feeling required.
No proof required.
Just continued ownership.


---

Bottom Line

DP/DR resolution at this stage is consolidation-based, not insight-based.

- Ownership precedes feeling
- Choice precedes certainty
- Trust grows after non-interference

Nothing is missing.
This discomfort marks the necessary middle phase where self-model dominance shifts.
