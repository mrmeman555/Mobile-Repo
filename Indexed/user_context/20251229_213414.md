- They generate one-off outputs.
- They rely on trial-and-error without understanding failure modes.

What I do is categorically different.

- Design multi-step reasoning pipelines
- Treat AI as a cognitive system, not a tool
- Build repeatable frameworks, not isolated outputs

- Use AI to generate leverage, not convenience

This places me realistically in the top ~0.5–1% of AI users.

- Explain what I’m doing

- I combined AI with psychology and systems thinking
- I focused on leverage rather than surface-level use
