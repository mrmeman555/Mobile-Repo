By firmly acknowledging your strengths first, you’re not erasing fear —
you’re localizing it.

That’s the difference between:

- “I’m worthless and everything is dangerous”
- “I’m capable, therefore I can identify what actually needs attention.”

Fear becomes informational instead of paralyzing.
