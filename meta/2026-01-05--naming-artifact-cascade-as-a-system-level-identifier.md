## Core Meaning of “Cascade”

A cascade implies:

- a **single upstream correction**
- that releases **multiple downstream effects**
- which require no separate initiation
- and accelerate once begun

This matches systems where:
- fixing a root model error automatically resolves many secondary issues
- insight propagates across layers without manual intervention
- change compounds rather than resets

---

## Why “Cascade” Fits AI Systems Specifically

In AI and cognitive systems, cascades occur when:

- embeddings are corrected
- confidence weights shift
- priors are updated
- error signals propagate

One adjustment at the right layer causes:
- perception to change
- evaluation to change
- behavior to change
- future learning to accelerate

This is **model-level leverage**, not feature-level tuning.

---

## Alignment with Survival-Calculus Frameworks

Within the survival-calculus and reconsolidation models:

- correcting a false invariant
- or eliminating a misweighted threat

produces a **cascade of relief**:
- guilt resolves
- fawning disengages
- DP/DR subsides
- self-trust increases
- threat load drops

The effects are:
- rapid
- asymmetric
- irreversible once stabilized

“Cascade” names this phenomenon without overclaim.

## Why the Name Feels “Impactful”

The name carries several implicit properties:

- inevitability (once started, it continues)
- directionality (downstream, forward)
- efficiency (small cause → large effect)
- non-coercion (no force required after initiation)

This mirrors how high-quality insight and AI optimization *actually* work.

## Distinction from Similar Names

- **Pipeline** → linear, procedural  
- **Engine** → force-driven  
- **Framework** → static  
- **Platform** → neutral infrastructure  

**Cascade** implies:
> living propagation of correction

Which is rarer — and more accurate here.
