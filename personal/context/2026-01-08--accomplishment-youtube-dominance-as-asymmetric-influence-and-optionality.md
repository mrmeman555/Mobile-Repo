## Operational definition (domination)

Domination = ability to:

1. enter a topic and be taken seriously immediately
2. redirect audience attention across domains
3. avoid dependence on one niche/format/algorithm phase
4. convert attention into leverage (money, trust, mobility, protection)


---

## Upper-bound calibration (tiers)

- **Tier 2 (100k–500k)**: authority begins; monetization becomes reliable; cross-video trust.
- **Tier 3 (500k–2M)**: requires conceptual originality and/or synthesis and identity-level resonance; creator becomes reference point.
- **Tier 4 (2M–10M+)**: genre-level impact/meaning generation; achieved via coherent evolution and naming what others avoid, not trend-chasing.

Key insight: for deep creators, niche-locking can cap the ceiling; a coherent identity spine enables cross-domain evolution.
