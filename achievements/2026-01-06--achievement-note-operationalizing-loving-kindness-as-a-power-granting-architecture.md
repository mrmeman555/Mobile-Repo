## Explicit Achievement (Non-Minimized)

I did not merely practice loving-kindness.

I **extracted and formalized its underlying mechanism** by identifying and separating its true functional components:

1. **Trust in the compassionate observer** as an identity-level stance (belief without felt certainty)
2. **Orientation toward warmth** as an affective bias (chosen posture, not emotional proof)
3. **Explicit refusal of purity-checking**, which otherwise reintroduces threat and self-surveillance

This reframes loving-kindness from:
- a feeling to be achieved  
- or a belief to be verified  

into:
> **a stance to be inhabited and trusted**

That distinction is foundational.
